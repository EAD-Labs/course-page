IIT Bombay • ET617

Educational App Design

A project-driven educational technology course focused on client-based software design, agile development, educational innovation, and iterative product creation.

Course Vision

ET617 enables students to collaboratively design and develop educational applications for real clients using agile methodologies, user-centered design, and iterative development processes.

Real-World Projects

Students work with external clients and educational stakeholders on authentic EdTech problems.

Agile Development

Teams learn iterative software development practices including prototyping, sprint reviews, and feedback loops.

Collaborative Design

Focus on interdisciplinary teamwork, requirement analysis, and educational interface design.

Semester Roadmap

A structured journey from client onboarding to final deployment and reflection.

Pre-Semester

Client Engagement

Identify and onboard project partners, define project ideas, and establish collaboration expectations.

Week 1

Course Orientation

Introduce course workflow, assessment, examples, and project ecosystem.

Week 2

Software Design Foundations

Agile methodologies, educational technology frameworks, and interaction logging concepts.

Week 3

Team Formation

Students form groups, select projects, and initiate stakeholder coordination.

Week 4–5

Requirement Analysis

Teams conduct client meetings, document requirements, and prepare project plans.

Week 6–7

Prototype Development

Early application prototypes are built, evaluated, and iteratively improved.

Mid-Semester

Formal Review

Prototype demonstrations and feedback-driven evaluations.

Week 8–12

Iterative Development

Refinement, usability improvements, integration, testing, and documentation.

End Semester

Final Showcase

Final presentations, project reports, and evaluation symposium with clients and faculty.

Course Infrastructure

GitHub Organization

Centralized repositories for collaborative development, issue tracking, and version control.

WhatsApp Community

Fast communication channel for announcements, coordination, and peer collaboration.

Project Documentation

Includes MoM templates, reports, presentations, and feedback systems.

Building Educational Technology with Real Impact

ET617 combines instructional design, software engineering, user research, and stakeholder collaboration to create meaningful educational applications that address authentic learning needs.
