IIT Bombay • ET617

Educational App Design

A project-based course where students design and develop educational applications in collaboration with real clients using agile and iterative development practices.

Course Overview

ET617 focuses on educational technology design, collaborative software development, stakeholder engagement, and user-centered learning experiences.

Client-Based Projects

Students work directly with external clients to solve authentic educational problems.

Agile Workflow

Teams follow iterative development processes with continuous feedback and refinement.

Collaborative Learning

Students learn teamwork, communication, documentation, and software planning.

Semester Timeline

The course progresses from project onboarding to final product presentation.

Pre-Semester

Client Engagement

Identify clients, collect project ideas, and finalize project briefs.

Week 1

Course Orientation

Introduction to course structure, evaluation, and project expectations.

Week 2

Software Design Concepts

Agile methodology, educational technology frameworks, and logging assignments.

Week 3

Team Formation & Project Allocation

Students form teams and select projects with instructor guidance.

Week 4–5

Requirement Analysis

Teams conduct meetings with clients and prepare requirement documents.

Week 6–10

Prototype Development

Develop and refine prototypes using client and peer feedback.

Week 11–12

Testing & Integration

Finalize products, perform testing, and prepare presentations and reports.

End of Semester

Final Presentation

Showcase completed projects before faculty and external stakeholders.

Learning Through Real Design Practice

ET617 combines educational technology, software development, collaboration, and design thinking to help students build impactful educational applications.
